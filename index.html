This is League of Animes

League of Animes (LoA) is the newest release fast-paced MOBA 2D action platformer. 3 Lanes. 6x6 Matches. 2 Bosses. The mission is simple, destroy the enemy base.

About LoA

Watch the video below for a GamePlay experience

Don't let your precious knowledge from previous MOBA games go to waste. LoA implements concepts of damage, stun, slow, poison, silence, critical, bash. Everything that you always been used to: towers, bots, runes, items and bosses.

Ok, but what's new besides the 2D environment?

  • Anime Style Characters
  • 4 Magics (5 Levels each)
  • About 20 minutes long matches
  • Beautiful animation effects
  • Planning to reach 20 Animes ASAP
  • NO LAG - Servers all over the world

This game is all about having fun without wasting a lot of time

Since the creation of Dota inside Warcraft 3 lots of players got addicted to playing the Defence of the Ancients style, later called MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena). Later a bunch of games were created following the same pattern.

In my experience as a player I saw the necessity of having control over the time I spent playing this style of game. I'm also a huge fan of animes, and then I thought... How about merging the 2 worlds into 1, creating a "health-friendly" game that has the essence I'm used to and the style I like the most.

I believe you got the point. MOBA=Good, 2D=Good, Animes=Good. Fast-paced=Good.

This is also my dream of becoming a game developer coming to reality, hope you guys enjoy.

Our work

On time delivery

Our team is composed by professionals that work hard to deliver all content in the right time.

Scalability

The structure of the game was developed in a scalable way to grow without bugs. The programmer staff of LoA have more than 5 years in game programming and 2 in Unity programming.

Constant character creation

Our team will be working on providing new Animes at all times, we believe that the total amount of Animes is very important for the success of the game.

Ranking system

The ranking system will allow you to compare your ability with everyone. We will evaluate ranking from different regions and clans.

Multiplayer realtime cloud-based system

All matches are hosted in servers running all over the world in the Cloud, which means no lagging.

Special server for SouthAmerica

Since the Cloud Server provider didn't cover the SouthAmerican area we hosted our own server in São Paulo exclusivelly for South American users. The ping in Rio is around 40ms.

Help us

Here we believe the player comes first. We are counting on you to improve our game. Donate or contribute playing the Alfa, or Closed Beta, or Open Beta releases.

Console integration

We plan to create consoles releases for PlayStation, PSVita, XBox and maybe more, as soon as we are ready for it.
